Digital Product Access Policy

Access Delivery

Because this is a digital product, no physical shipment takes place. After completing your purchase, access is delivered automatically through the Hotmart platform.

You will receive an email from Hotmart with your login details and instructions to access your content. In most cases, access is delivered instantly. In rare cases, delivery may take up to 5 minutes.

If You Did Not Receive Access

If you do not receive your access email, please check:

  • Your spam or junk folder
  • If the email used at checkout is correct
  • Your Hotmart account (using the same purchase email)

If access still does not appear, contact our support team and we will assist you.

Refund Policy

Refunds for digital products follow the guarantee period displayed on the checkout page. If your product includes a guarantee (e.g., 7, 15, or 30 days), you may request a refund directly through Hotmart.

How to Request a Refund

You can request a refund using Hotmart’s official refund portal:

https://refund.hotmart.com/

Enter the email used during purchase and follow the instructions. Once the refund is approved, your access to the digital content will be automatically revoked.

Wrong Email or Incorrect Information

If you entered your email incorrectly during purchase, your access may not reach you. Please contact our support team with:

  • Full Name
  • Purchase Email
  • Correct Email
  • Order Number (if available)

We will update your access credentials as quickly as possible.
